world Cricket Grounds, Stadiums & Venues

Following is a photographic Google satellite-image rendition and visual imagery, including street location maps (where available) of Test cricket grounds, ODI grounds and First Class stadia - plus major venues in all test playing and major non-test playing nations. If you wish to add a cricket ground, cricket stadium or cricket venue, please contact us. We are aiming to include all major cricket stadiums in the world, including non-test playng nations, so do visit this page often, and feel free to suggest anything either missing or in need of correction. Below, you will find a till-now comprehensive list of stadiums and grounds that we have mapped so far.
