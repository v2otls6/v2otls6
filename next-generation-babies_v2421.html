baggygreenmania
The National Under17 Cricket Championships, the first step along the pathway to a coveted Baggy Green, kicked off this week. Teams from all States and Territories plus a CA X1 rep. side will take part. Many of Australia's finest past and present cricketers excelled at these championships. A few names to watch out for this year are the NSW Blues youngest ever rookie Jason Sangha (stand corrected), the son of former great Steve Waugh, Austin and Mackenzie Harvey, the nephew of Ian 'the freak' Harvey. Brisbane and Maroochydore will host the week-long tournament. www.crickety.com
